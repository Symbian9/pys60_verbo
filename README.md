# Verbo
###### Text reader based on RSVP technology for PyS60 platform

#### Supported formats:
- txt
- fb2

#### Controls
- 5 - pause

(on pause)
- 5 - start
- 4 - next word
- 6 - previous word
- 7 - rewind forward on 10 words
- 8 - rewind backward on 10 words
- "*" (star) - increase speed
- "#" (hash) - decrease speed
